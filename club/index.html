What's the deal?

ByteBlitz is a timed coding competition where participants solve a series of algorithmic problems across increasing difficulty levels. You get three hours, one machine, and zero excuses. Whether you're a first-year testing the waters or a final-year sharpening your edge — everyone's on an equal footing here.

Problems span data structures, logic, and the occasional curveball. Come prepared. Come hungry.
